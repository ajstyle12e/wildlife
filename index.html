How We Can Protect Wildlife

We all have a role in preserving the biodiversity around us. Here are some ways we can help:

  • Support local wildlife sanctuaries and parks.
  • Reduce habitat destruction by minimizing deforestation.
  • Plant native species to support local pollinators like bees and butterflies.
  • Volunteer for clean-up events and habitat restoration.

Conservation Tips

  • Reduce plastic use and recycle.
  • Plant native species in your garden.
  • Support local wildlife reserves.
  • Join community clean-up events.
